Hello! My name is Kelly Chen (they/them). I am an MIT '21-22 alum who studied pure mathematics and chemistry. I am currently working as a bioethics research fellow at the National Institutes of Health, and I will be pursuing a chemistry PhD this fall.

You may also know me from...

  • flow and fire performance arts, which I started doing at MIT in 2017. I was previously the president of the MIT Spinning Arts Club and occasionally still assist in my alum capacity. I mostly spin rope dart and poi, and I dabble in fire-eating and breathing.
  • spinning arts prop construction, which I have done in both personal and professional capacities. I custom-built all of the fire and glow equipment that I use. Feel free to reach out with questions!
  • other maker projects. I am currently working with my friend and favorite sled dog Audrey Cui on a custom pair of Damascus steel wedding bands for a dear friend of mine and her husband, who got married at Chuck E. Cheese.
  • union organizing. Collective action is collective power. ;)
